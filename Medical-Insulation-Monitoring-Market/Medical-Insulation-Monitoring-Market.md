<li>The healthcare industry is, in fact, one of the most strictly regulated industries worldwide, there is a plethora of standards and regulations laid down by almost every legal entity worldwide, including the U. Thus, insulation monitoring systems are valuable for achieving these outcomes because they assist in identifying insulation breakdowns that can endanger patients&rsquo; lives. The regulatory pressure in this case makes healthcare providers seek for good insulation monitoring systems that they do not incur penalties, product recalls, and possible litigations. Thus, the demand for compliance with high safety requirements is one of the strong stimulants for the development of the medical insulation monitoring market.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Increasing Complexity and Sophistication of Medical Devices</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Contemporary medical appliances are now developing complexity with more electrical components and involving advance technologies in order to improve its functionality and the amity of the patients. This complexity increases the probability of electrical insulation breakdowns, which in turn jeopardizes the proper functioning of the devices and thus patient lives and treatment remedies. In such a landscape, insulation monitoring systems play the key role of constantly supervising the status of electrical insulation in medical devices. Such systems aid the healthcare provider with real time alerts and diagnostic on the insulation, allowing them avoid intermissions and guarantee safety of the devices. Technological advancement in medical devices and electronics has made their insulation sophisticated and that creates the need for reliable insulation monitoring hence fostering market growth.</li>
</ul>
